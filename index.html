Major Projects:

Destiny 2 is an MMO-FPS built by Bungie, which I've worked on as an activity designer since January 2022.

For Destiny I've owned numerous pieces of activity content across the game (raids, dungeons, missions), focusing on long-form highly replayable missions, built for highly engaged players who want a challenging cooperative experience.

Mirastell is a 2D Action-RPG with a cross between turn-based and bullet-hell combat which I built over the course of over 3 years. It's available now on steam.

Who I am
